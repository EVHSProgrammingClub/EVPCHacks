About The Hackathon

EVPC Hacks I is a district-wide high school hackathon with the goal of uniting like-minded learners and thinkers, to collaborate on meaningful projects, during these dire times.

While COVID-19 has made it difficult to have such events, we have resolved to provide opportunities for our community members to both learn to and practice code. Weâ€™re therefore incredibly excited to announce that we plan on hosting our very own online hackathon this March.
